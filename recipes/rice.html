White Rice Recipe

Description

This recipe is one of the easiest ones you will find to make some of the best carbohydrates to implement in your diet. Not only is white rice cheap and widely available, it is also a complex carb that will provide ample energy without putting the pounds on.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup White Rice
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Salt shaker

Steps

  1. Measure out 1/2 cup of white rice
  2. Add to rice cooker with 1 cup of water
  3. Activate rice cooker and wait
  4. Upon completion, add salt and enjoy your meal
