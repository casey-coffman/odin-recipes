Preseasoned Chicken Recipe

Description

Chicken is one of the best proteins due to its low fat content and high protein content without carrying the negative side effect of red meat. This is made even better by being preseasoned so you have to do even less work and can easily meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Preseasoned Chicken
  • 1/8 Stick of Butter

Steps

  1. Chop 2lbs of chicken into 1 inch cubes
  2. Heat pan to med-high and add 1/8 stick of butter
  3. Add chicken and put lid on pan, occasionally stirring
  4. Remove chicken when fully cooked and enjoy your meal
