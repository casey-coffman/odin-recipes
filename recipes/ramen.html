Microwave Cup Ramen Recipe

Description

This storebought ramen comes pre-packaged in a cup for easy consumption. No cooking skills are required thanks to its microwaveability and small form factor. If you want to spice it up you may add some chicken or other meat to it or just eat it plain.

Ingredients

  • Cup ramen
  • Protein (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the top halfway back
  2. Add water to inside line near top
  3. Place in microwave for 1 minute on high
  4. Remove from microwave and optionally add protein
  5. Let cool and enjoy your meal
